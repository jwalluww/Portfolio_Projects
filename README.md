# Hi, I'm Justin 👋

I'm a data scientist with 10+ years of experience solving business problems using statistics, causal inference, and Bayesian methods. I work remotely at a tech startup and spend most of my time building models for businesses across several industries.

I'm especially drawn to problems where the answer isn't obvious and the data is messy. Experimentation design, causal questions, uncertainty quantification, that's where I thrive.

---

## What I work on

My focus areas, roughly in order of obsession:

- **Bayesian inference** — hierarchical models, posterior predictive checks, prior sensitivity, PyMC
- **Causal inference** — uplift modeling, diff-in-differences, synthetic control, quasi-experiments
- **Experimentation** — A/B testing, sequential testing, CUPED, experiment framework design
- **Marketing & growth analytics** — MMM, CLV, segmentation, propensity modeling
- **Optimization** — linear programming for resource allocation and pricing decisions
